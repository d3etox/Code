# Prion dynamics modelling

> Numerical modelling of prion proliferation mechanisms, responsible for spongiform encephalopathy (Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease).

**Context**: M1 project (Mathematics and modelling of living systems).

## What it does
Study of a system of differential equations describing the interaction between the healthy protein **PrPC** and its pathological form **PrPSc**, which replicates by "contaminating" the healthy form.
- Proof of existence/positivity of solutions on an admissible domain
- Logistic growth model (analytical vs numerical solution)
- Numerical integration of the system and study of population dynamics

## Stack
Python (NumPy, Matplotlib), Jupyter.

## Files
- `Modélisation_Prion_CharlesFabre.ipynb` — main notebook (theory + simulations)
- `test.py`, `test.ipynb` — prototypes
