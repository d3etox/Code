# Optimization & root finding

> Optimization algorithms and solving of non-linear equations.

**Context**: Optimization course labs (Master in Mathematical Engineering).

## What it does
- **Newton's method** for root finding (`f(x)=0`) and study of quadratic convergence.
- Visualization of functions and their roots.
- Successive labs exploring different optimization methods.

## Stack
Python (NumPy, Matplotlib), Jupyter.

## Files
- `TP1.ipynb`, `TP2.ipynb`, `TP3.ipynb` — course labs
- `test.py` — Newton implementation

## Run
Open the notebooks in Jupyter.
